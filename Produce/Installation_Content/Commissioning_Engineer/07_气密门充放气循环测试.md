# Airtight Door Inflation-Deflation Cycle Test: Repeated Mechanical Cycle Testing for Seal Longevity and Performance Verification

## 适用受众
Commissioning_Engineer

## 写作角度说明
气密门充放气循环测试是调试工程师验证门体在连续工作状态下的机械性能和密封可靠性的关键测试。通过执行多次完整的充放气循环，确认门体的运动机构、密封系统和控制系统在重复工况下的稳定性。

## 核心论点 / 洞察
Running a cycle test at nominal air supply pressure — without testing at minimum supply pressure (4 bar when other doors are open) — validates performance under ideal conditions but not under the degraded supply condition that occurs during multi-door operation.

## 参考素材
- Cycle test procedure: perform 20 consecutive inflation-deflation cycles → record inflation time, deflation time, and seal pressure at each cycle → monitor for seal pressure degradation
- Acceptance criteria: all cycles complete without fault alarm → inflation time ≤5 seconds (per BS-01-IAD-1 spec) → deflation time ≤5 seconds → minimum seal pressure at cycle 20 ≥0.20 MPa (80% of initial value)
- Supply pressure variation test: repeat cycle test at minimum supply pressure (4 bar) → verify system maintains functional operation → record any performance degradation
- Seal wear assessment: compare seal pressure at cycle 1 and cycle 20 → compression set calculated per ISO 1856 (acceptable ≤15%)
- Report documentation: cycle log with timestamp for each cycle, pressure trend chart, as-found/as-left comparison, pass/fail determination with signature
