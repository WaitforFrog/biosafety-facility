# Installed Surface Cleaning and Protection: Post-Installation Protection Protocol for Stainless Steel Equipment

## 适用受众
Installation_Technician

## 写作角度说明
安装完成面的清洁与防护是交付前的最后工作。安装技师需要清除所有安装痕迹（焊点、打磨痕迹、油污），对不锈钢表面进行钝化处理，并粘贴保护膜防止后续施工阶段的划伤。

## 核心论点 / 洞察
Leaving protective film on stainless steel surfaces through the full construction phase — rather than removing it within 30 days of installation — creates adhesive migration stains that require professional polishing to remove.

## 参考素材
- Cleaning procedure: remove all welding scale, grinding marks, and construction debris → degrease with 5% neutral detergent → rinse with deionized water → apply stainless steel passivation solution (10-15% citric acid) → rinse and dry
- Passivation specification: citric acid passivation per ASTM A967, contact time 20-60 minutes, temperature 20-30°C, rinse with pH-neutral water
- Protection: apply temporary protective film (50-80 μm polyethylene, low-adhesive acrylic adhesive) immediately after cleaning, remove within 30 days of installation
- Scratch prevention: install corner guards on exposed edges, apply adhesive felt pads at contact points
- Final inspection: 100% visual inspection under 500 lux illumination, no scratches visible at 1 meter distance, no fingerprints, no adhesive residue
