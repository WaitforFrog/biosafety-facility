# Foundation Levelness and Embedded Part Verification: Pre-Installation Dimensional Survey of Wall Openings and Concrete Bases

## 适用受众
Installation_Technician

## 写作角度说明
设备基础水平度和预埋件位置的复核是安装前的关键测量工作。安装技师需要在设备就位前，用水平仪、经纬仪和钢卷尺复核基础面的平整度、预留洞口的尺寸和预埋件的位置，确保满足设备安装的尺寸要求。

## 核心论点 / 洞察
Measuring wall opening dimensions only at the visible face — without checking the opening cross-section at mid-depth — misses the condition where the opening narrows due to concrete formwork bow, preventing equipment insertion.

## 参考素材
- Levelness survey: use digital precision level (resolution 0.01 mm/m), measure across foundation at minimum 4 points, acceptance: ≤2 mm/m in any direction
- Opening dimension check: measure width and height at top, middle, and bottom of opening (6 measurements total), check diagonal dimensions, acceptance: nominal dimension +0/-5 mm
- Embedded part survey: locate all embedded plates, conduit stubs, and ground studs, measure positions relative to opening centerline, mark on temporary survey drawing
- Floor flatness: 2-meter straightedge test per ACI 117, maximum gap 3 mm under straightedge, fill low spots with epoxy grout before anchor installation
- Anchor position verification: confirm all structural anchors are installed at specified locations, measure embedment depth, verify no interference with embedded conduit
