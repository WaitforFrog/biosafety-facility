Q: What are the most common mechanical compression seal failure modes, and how do maintenance intervals vary across suppliers?

Suppliers specifying medical-grade silicone with compression set values below 15% (per ASTM D395) achieve 5-7 year seal lifespans, while lower-grade materials require replacement every 2-3 years. Maintenance protocols should include annual visual inspection, biannual pressure decay verification, and seal replacement at first sign of permanent deformation—typically indicated by pressure loss exceeding 25% during routine testing.
