# Brand Equity in Technical Sourcing: Why Reference Installations Matter in B2B Equipment Selection

## 适用受众
CEO

## 写作角度说明
在B2B技术采购中，品牌信誉和标杆客户案例是 CEO 评估供应商的重要维度。本角度分析标杆客户对供应商可信度的背书作用，帮助 CEO 建立客户背书评估框架。

## 核心论点 / 洞察
Reference installations at prestigious institutions—national laboratories, leading pharmaceutical companies, research universities—serve as credible third-party validation that no sales pitch can replicate.

## 参考素材
- Government and research institution references: Chinese CDC, Wuhan Institute of Virology, provincial P3 laboratories
- Pharmaceutical industry references: WuXi AppTec, Beijing Ganli, Changchun Bcht, Liaoning Yikang
- Animal health P3 facilities: Inner Mongolia Jinyu, Xinjiang Tiankang, Jilin Heyuan
- International market validation: export to Russia, Singapore, Southeast Asia, Middle East
- Site visit opportunities: reference installation tours, operator interviews, performance data access

## 关键词
Reference Installations, Brand Credibility, Case Studies, Industry Recognition, Peer Validation, Reputation Management
