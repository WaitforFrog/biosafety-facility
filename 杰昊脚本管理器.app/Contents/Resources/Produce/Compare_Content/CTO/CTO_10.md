# Third-Party Certification and Validation Documentation: The Technical Due Diligence Checklist

## 适用受众
CTO

## 写作角度说明
供应商的技术认证和验证文件是 CTO 进行供应商评估的核心依据。本角度帮助 CTO 建立系统化的技术尽调清单，识别真正具备技术实力的供应商。

## 核心论点 / 洞察
Third-party certifications and validation documentation—test reports, 3Q protocols, ISO certificates—are not marketing materials but objective evidence of a supplier's technical credibility.

## 参考素材
- Third-party testing organizations: CNSA (China National Supervision and Testing Center), ICAS, SEDA testing capabilities
- 3Q Documentation: IQ (Installation Qualification), OQ (Operational Qualification), PQ (Performance Qualification)
- ISO certification verification: ISO 9001:2015, ISO 14001:2015, ISO 45001:2018 scope and validity
- Pressure decay test reports: test conditions, acceptance criteria, pass/fail criteria
- Material certificates: mill test reports for 304/316L stainless steel, seal material certifications

## 关键词
Third-Party Testing, 3Q Validation, ISO Certification, CNSA, ICAS, Technical Due Diligence, Quality Documentation
