# Pressure Decay Testing Methodology: Establishing Pass/Fail Criteria for Airtight Seal Performance

## 适用受众
CTO

## 写作角度说明
压力衰减测试是验证气密门和传递窗密封性能的金标准方法，但测试参数的微小差异会导致完全不同的判定结果。本角度帮助 CTO 深入理解压力衰减测试的方法论，包括测试压力设置、持续时间、泄漏率计算和判定标准，为供应商技术评估提供客观依据。

## 核心论点 / 洞察
Pressure decay testing methodology—test pressure selection, duration calibration, and leak rate calculation—reveals the technical rigor of a supplier's quality assurance system; a vendor who cannot explain their test parameters cannot guarantee seal performance.

## 参考素材
- Test pressure selection: -500Pa (negative pressure) vs +500Pa (positive pressure), rationale for negative pressure preference
- Duration requirements: 20-minute standard test (ISO 14644-1), extended 30-minute test for high-containment (ABSL-3/4)
- Pressure measurement precision: differential pressure transmitter accuracy (±1Pa), calibration frequency
- Leak rate calculation: ΔP/Δt method, volume correction, temperature compensation
- Pass/fail criteria: ≤250Pa pressure loss for standard cleanroom, ≤125Pa for P3/P4 biosafety applications
- JIEHAO's National Inspection Center (NCSA) test reports as third-party validation methodology reference
- False positive prevention: sensor drift correction, ambient temperature variation control
- Repeatability verification: consecutive test results consistency, batch sampling protocol
