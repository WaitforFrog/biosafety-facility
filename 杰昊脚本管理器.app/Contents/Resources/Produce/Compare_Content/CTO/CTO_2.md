# Material Selection for Corrosion Resistance: 304 vs 316L Stainless Steel in H2O2 Environments

## 适用受众
CTO

## 写作角度说明
在生物安全高压灭菌环境中，材料选择直接决定了设备寿命和维护周期。本角度深入分析304与316L不锈钢在过氧化氢(H2O2)灭菌环境中的耐腐蚀性能差异，帮助 CTO 建立材料选型的技术评估框架。

## 核心论点 / 洞察
Material grade selection—316L over 304—represents a critical decision point that determines equipment longevity and maintenance costs in H2O2 sterilization environments.

## 参考素材
- Chemical compatibility comparison: H2O2 sterilization (concentration 35%), formaldehyde sterilization, common disinfectants
- Corrosion mechanism analysis: chromium oxide layer stability, molybdenum addition effects
- Temperature range performance: -30°C to +50°C operating environment specifications
- Industry standards: ASTM A240/A240M for stainless steel sheet/plate specifications
- JIEHAO's 304/316 material options as evidence of application-specific customization capability

Stainless Steel 316L, Corrosion Resistance, H2O2 Sterilization, Material Science, ASTM A240, Molybdenum
