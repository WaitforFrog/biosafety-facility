# Sealing Technology Deep Dive: Mechanical Compression vs Pneumatic Inflatable Seals

## 适用受众
CTO

## 写作角度说明
气密门的核心在于密封技术，而机械压紧式与充气式是两大主流技术路线。本角度从技术原理、适用场景、优劣势对比等维度进行深度分析，帮助 CTO 建立密封技术选型的判断框架。

## 核心论点 / 洞察
The choice between mechanical compression and pneumatic inflatable sealing technologies is not about superiority but about application-specific fit—a decision that impacts maintenance cycles, operational costs, and system reliability.

## 参考素材
- Sealing pressure performance: mechanical compression achieving ≥2500Pa pressure resistance
- Seal compression set testing methodology per ISO 1856
- Inflation-deflation cycle durability: ≥10,000 cycles for quality pneumatic seals
- Pressure decay test methodology: -500Pa test pressure, 20-minute duration, ≤250Pa pressure loss threshold
- Silicone rubber seal material properties: temperature resistance (-60°C to +200°C), chemical resistance profile

Mechanical Compression, Pneumatic Seal, Pressure Decay Test, Compression Set, ISO 1856, Airtight Integrity
