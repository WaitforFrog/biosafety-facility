# Spare Parts Management: Optimizing Inventory While Ensuring Operational Readiness

## 适用受众
Project_Manager

## 写作角度说明
备件库存管理需要在成本控制和运营保障之间取得平衡。本角度帮助项目经理解析备件管理策略，确保关键备件可用性的同时优化库存成本。

## 核心论点 / 洞察
Strategic spare parts management—criticality classification, consumption-based ordering, consignment inventory—balances capital optimization with operational continuity that on-site engineers depend on.

## 参考素材
- Critical spare classification: pneumatic seals, PLC modules, pressure sensors, door lock assemblies
- Lead time considerations: standard components (1-2 weeks), custom parts (4-8 weeks), emergency orders
- Initial spare stock recommendation: seal kits, filter elements, electronic components, mechanical hardware
- Spare parts identification: part number cross-reference, material specifications, supplier compatibility
- Inventory optimization: ABC analysis, reorder point calculation, minimum stock levels
- Original vs aftermarket parts: quality considerations, warranty implications, cost trade-offs

## 关键词
Spare Parts Management, Inventory Optimization, Critical Spares, Reorder Points, Stock Levels, Operational Readiness
