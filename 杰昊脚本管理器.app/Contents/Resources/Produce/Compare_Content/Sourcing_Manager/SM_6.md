# Single vs Multi-Source Sourcing Strategy: Balancing Cost and Supply Chain Resilience

## 适用受众
Sourcing_Manager

## 写作角度说明
单源还是多源采购是战略决策，涉及成本、风险、供应商关系等多重因素。本角度帮助采购专家权衡利弊，制定适合企业风险偏好的采购策略。

## 核心论点 / 洞察
Single-source vs multi-source decisions—based on spend volume, supply risk, supplier criticality—should be made explicitly rather than by default, balancing cost optimization with supply continuity protection.

## 参考素材
- Single-source advantages: volume consolidation, relationship depth, technology roadmap access
- Single-source risks: supply disruption, negotiation leverage loss, quality dependency
- Multi-source benefits: competition, supply resilience, risk distribution
- Multi-source challenges: volume dilution, quality variance, management complexity
- Decision framework: category classification (strategic/leverage/critical/routine), sourcing model selection
- Qualification management: approved supplier list maintenance, new supplier development pipeline

## 关键词
Sourcing Strategy, Single Source, Multi Source, Supply Chain Resilience, Risk Management, Supplier Relationship, Category Strategy
