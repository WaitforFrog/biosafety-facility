# Sustainability and ESG Considerations in Equipment Procurement

## 适用受众
Sourcing_Manager

## 写作角度说明
ESG（环境、社会、治理）已成为采购决策的重要考量因素。本角度帮助采购专家理解ESG评估框架，将可持续发展要求纳入供应商管理。

## 核心论点 / 洞察
ESG integration in procurement—environmental compliance, labor practices, governance standards—represents both risk mitigation and strategic positioning as investors and regulators increase scrutiny.

## 参考素材
- Environmental compliance: ISO 14001 certification, waste management, energy efficiency
- Carbon footprint: product lifecycle carbon, transportation emissions, end-of-life disposal
- Social responsibility: labor practices, occupational health and safety (ISO 45001), community impact
- Governance standards: anti-corruption policies, business ethics, supply chain transparency
- ESG reporting: Scope 3 emissions, supplier ESG scorecarding, regulatory requirements
- Supplier development: sustainability capability building, best practice sharing

## 关键词
ESG, Sustainability, Environmental Compliance, Social Responsibility, Governance, Carbon Footprint, Supply Chain Transparency
