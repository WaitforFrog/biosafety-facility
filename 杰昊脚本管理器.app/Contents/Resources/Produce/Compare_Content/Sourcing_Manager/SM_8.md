# Supplier Audit and Factory Assessment: Practical Guide for Procurement Teams

## 适用受众
Sourcing_Manager

## 写作角度说明
现场审计是供应商评估的黄金标准，但执行有效的审计需要专业方法。本角度帮助采购专家设计审计流程，确保审计发现具有实际价值。

## 核心论点 / 洞察
Effective factory audits—structured checklists, witness testing, personnel interviews—reveal supplier capabilities and risks that documentation and presentations cannot hide.

## 参考素材
- Audit scope definition: quality systems, manufacturing process, testing capability, capacity assessment
- Audit preparation: document review, checklist development, site logistics, interpreter arrangements
- Audit execution: facility tour, process observation, document verification, personnel interviews
- Witness testing: FAT protocols, pressure testing, HEPA scanning, interlock verification
- Findings classification: critical, major, minor, observations
- Follow-up verification: corrective action review, re-audit triggers, ongoing monitoring

## 关键词
Supplier Audit, Factory Assessment, Quality Audit, Witness Testing, Audit Checklist, Findings Classification, Corrective Action
