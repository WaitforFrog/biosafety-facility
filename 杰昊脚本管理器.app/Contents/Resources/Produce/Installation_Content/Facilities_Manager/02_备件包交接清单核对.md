# Spare Parts Kit Handover Verification: Confirming Completeness and Storage Requirements for Installed Equipment

## 适用受众
Facilities_Manager

## 写作角度说明
备件包交接是设备从安装转向运维的关键环节。设施经理需要核对备件包清单的完整性，确认备件存储条件和管理规范，并建立备件台账，确保运维阶段能够及时获取所需备件。

## 核心论点 / 洞察
Facilities that do not establish a spare parts inventory tagging system within 30 days of equipment handover experience 3× longer mean time to repair on emergency seal replacement calls.

## 参考素材
- Standard spare parts kit contents: pneumatic seal set (primary and secondary), fuse kit (all rated fuses), pressure sensor (spare differential pressure transmitter), door hinge bushings, gasket kit for control panel
- Storage requirements: sealed storage at 15-25°C, 40-60% RH, UV-protected, sealed original packaging, away from magnetic fields and vibration sources
- Handover verification procedure: physical count against packing list, photo documentation of each part, condition assessment (new in packaging vs. used), storage location assignment
- Inventory management: minimum stock levels per equipment type, reorder point calculation (based on mean time between failures), parts tracking by equipment serial number
- Documentation: signed handover form, spare parts inventory log with part numbers and quantities, recommended reorder suppliers and lead times
