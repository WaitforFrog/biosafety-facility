# Civil Foundation Levelness and Flatness Verification: Site Preparation Condition Survey Before Equipment Installation Commences

## 适用受众
Facilities_Manager

## 写作角度说明
土建基础平整度核查清单是设施经理在设备到场前对土建施工完成面进行全面检查的关键工作。通过系统性的现场核查，确保土建条件满足设备安装要求，避免设备到场后因基础问题无法安装。

## 核心论点 / 洞察
Accepting a civil foundation surface on visual inspection alone — without measuring floor flatness per ACI 117 tolerances — creates an unquantified risk of equipment misalignment that manifests only during commissioning.

## 参考素材
- Flatness survey: 2-meter straightedge test per ACI 117, maximum gap 3 mm, measure at minimum 9 points across the installation area
- Levelness survey: digital precision level at minimum 4 corners of installation area, acceptance ±2 mm/m
- Opening dimension verification: check wall opening dimensions match equipment drawing, measure at top/middle/bottom of opening (6 measurements), check diagonal dimensions
- Embedded part survey: locate all embedded plates, anchor channels, and conduit stubs, verify positions against structural drawing
- Moisture check: measure concrete surface moisture content (acceptable <4% by weight for epoxy coatings, <6% for most floor finishes), check for active leaks or water staining
- Document record: signed checklist with measured values, photographs of each measurement point, sign-off by civil contractor and client representative
