# Equipment Installation Space Net Dimension Verification: Confirming Available Clearance for Equipment Access and Maintenance

## 适用受众
Facilities_Manager

## 写作角度说明
设备安装空间净尺寸复核是设施经理确保设备到场后能够正确就位和维护的关键核查工作。通过测量现场的可用空间、通道宽度和吊顶高度，确认设备尺寸与现场条件匹配。

## 核心论点 / 洞察
Verifying the ceiling height against the architectural drawing — without physically measuring the actual ceiling height at the equipment location — misses the case where architectural drawings show design height, but the actual installed ceiling is 100-150 mm lower due to structure thickness deviations.

## 参考素材
- Available clearance check: measure ceiling height at equipment location → compare to equipment overall height + minimum rigging clearance (300 mm) → acceptance: actual height ≥ equipment height + 300 mm
- Access corridor width: measure corridor width along entire delivery route from receiving bay to equipment location → acceptance: corridor width ≥ equipment width + 600 mm for maneuvering
- Door clearance: measure all doors, elevators, and openings along delivery route → acceptance: door opening ≥ largest equipment dimension + 200 mm
- Turning radius: for equipment with irregular shapes, calculate minimum turning radius required → verify corridor has adequate turning clearance
- Maintenance clearance: verify required maintenance clearance is maintained (per manufacturer drawing) after equipment installation — for pass boxes: front clearance ≥800 mm for door swing and maintenance access; for doors: side clearance ≥600 mm for hinge clearance
- Record documentation: signed dimension survey with photographs at each measurement point, annotated layout drawing showing actual dimensions
