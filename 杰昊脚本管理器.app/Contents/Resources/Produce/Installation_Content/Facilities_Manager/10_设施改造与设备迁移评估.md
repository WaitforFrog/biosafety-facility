# Facility Upgrade and Equipment Relocation Assessment: Planning for Future Space Reconfiguration or Equipment Movement

## 适用受众
Facilities_Manager

## 写作角度说明
设施改造与设备迁移评估是设施经理规划未来设施升级或设备移位的预备性工作。虽然生物安全设备通常固定安装，但在设施改造期间可能需要临时移位或重新定位，需要预先评估可行性和成本。

## 核心论点 / 洞察
Assuming that a biosafety equipment unit can be relocated without re-qualification — because it was installed correctly the first time — ignores that relocation stress (transport, reinstallation, reconnection) requires IQ/OQ re-execution before operational use.

## 参考素材
- Relocation feasibility factors: equipment design (fixed vs. relocatable) → facility infrastructure dependencies (HVAC, BMS, electrical) → regulatory classification (BSL-2 vs. BSL-3) → decontamination requirements before relocation
- Decontamination requirements: VHP or formaldehyde fumigation required before any biosafety equipment is moved → certified decontamination report required before disassembly → waste disposal permit for contaminated components
- Re-qualification scope: full relocation typically requires IQ + OQ (not full IQ/OQ/PQ) → scope may be reduced if relocation is within same room and utilities are unchanged
- Cost factors: decontamination and re-installation typically 20-40% of original installation cost → re-qualification testing 15-25% of original commissioning cost → facility downtime during relocation
- Documentation for future planning: as-built drawings and photographs at sufficient detail to support future re-installation → disassembly procedure (ask manufacturer to provide if not in O&M manual)

## 关键词
Equipment Relocation, Facility Upgrade, Re-Qualification, Decontamination, Relocation Planning, Asset Mobility
