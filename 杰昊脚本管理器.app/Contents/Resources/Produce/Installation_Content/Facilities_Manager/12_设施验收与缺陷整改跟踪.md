# Facility Acceptance and Defect Rectification Tracking: Managing the Final Acceptance Process and Snagging List Resolution

## 适用受众
Facilities_Manager

## 写作角度说明
设施验收与缺陷整改跟踪是设施经理参与设备最终验收和跟踪遗留缺陷整改的核心工作。通过规范的验收流程和缺陷跟踪机制，确保设备以良好状态正式投入使用，所有问题都有闭环记录。

## 核心论点 / 洞察
Signing the facility acceptance certificate before all critical defects are resolved — with a verbal agreement that the defects will be addressed — creates a legal situation where the contractor has fulfilled their contractual obligation upon signature, regardless of any pending work.

## 参考素材
- Acceptance criteria definition: define acceptance criteria in the purchase/installation contract before project starts → criteria must be specific, measurable, and testable → avoid vague criteria like "satisfactory completion"
- Pre-acceptance inspection: full system walk-through → execute all acceptance test procedures → document all observations (normal/improvement/defect) → classify defects (critical/major/minor)
- Defect classification: critical = safety hazard or regulatory non-compliance → major = performance below specification → minor = cosmetic or convenience issue
- Rectification timeline: critical defects must be rectified before acceptance → major defects: agreed rectification period post-acceptance (typically 30-60 days) → minor defects: recorded but may be addressed in planned maintenance
- Acceptance certificate: issued only when all critical and major defects are resolved → certificate states acceptance is conditional on major defects resolved within agreed period → retain right to withhold final payment portion until all defects resolved
- Final acceptance: issued after all contractual defects are resolved → triggers warranty period start date
