# Seal Gasket Installation and Protection: Preserving Elastomer Integrity During and After Installation

## 适用受众
Installation_Technician

## 写作角度说明
密封件是气密门系统的核心易损件，安装过程中如果保护不当会直接影响密封性能。安装技师需要在安装期间和安装完成后对所有密封件进行保护，避免机械损伤、化学腐蚀和高温变形。

## 核心论点 / 洞察
Exposing EPDM and silicone seals to solvent-based cleaning agents — applied by the cleaning crew after installation — causes immediate compression set degradation that voids the seal's warranty and accelerates replacement cycles.

## 参考素材
- Seal material compatibility: EPDM seals incompatible with petroleum-based solvents, silicone seals sensitive to strong acids and bases, verify cleaning agent compatibility before use
- Protection during installation: cover seal groove with masking tape during grinding/welding nearby, remove protective film only after all finishing work is complete
- Temperature limits: EPDM operating range -30°C to +80°C, silicone -60°C to +200°C, avoid VHP exposure above 60% concentration at temperatures above 40°C
- Storage: store spare seals flat, not hanging, away from UV light and ozone sources, humidity 40-60% RH
- Handling: wear clean gloves when handling seals, never touch sealing surface with bare hands (skin oils cause premature aging)
