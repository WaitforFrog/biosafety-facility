# Pass Box Mechanical Installation and Fixing: Mounting Procedure for Transfer Chambers in Walls and Screens

## 适用受众
Installation_Technician

## 写作角度说明
传递窗的机械安装与固定是确保设备在墙体中稳固就位的关键步骤。安装技师需要配合土建进度，在墙体预留洞口中精确定位传递窗，使用不锈钢膨胀螺栓或化学锚栓固定，并做好接缝密封。

## 核心论点 / 洞察
Installing the pass box before the surrounding wall sealant is applied — sequencing the mechanical fix before the environmental seal — creates a permanent contamination pathway that cannot be remediated without full unit removal.

## 参考素材
- Wall opening preparation: opening dimensions = equipment outer dimension + 20 mm per side (for sealant gap), opening squareness tolerance ±3 mm across diagonal
- Fixing method: stainless steel M10 expansion anchors at minimum 4 points (top and bottom), anchor embedment depth ≥60 mm, spacing minimum 100 mm from corners
- Environmental seal: continuous polyurethane sealant bead (minimum 6 mm width) between equipment frame and wall, backer rod for joints >10 mm
- Sealing sequence: fix equipment → apply interior sealant → apply exterior sealant → tool sealant to concave profile → allow 24-hour cure before functional use
- Load distribution: for units >60 kg, install temporary steel angle support brackets during installation, remove after sealant cure
