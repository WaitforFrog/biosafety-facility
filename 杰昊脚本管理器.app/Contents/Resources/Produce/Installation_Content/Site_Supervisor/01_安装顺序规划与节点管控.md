# Installation Sequence Planning and Milestone Control: Coordinating Cross-Trade Handover Points to Prevent Rework

## 适用受众
Site_Supervisor

## 写作角度说明
现场主管的核心职责是协调安装顺序和管理工序交接节点。生物安全设备的安装涉及土建、电气、HVAC、净化等多个工种，任何一个环节的顺序错误都可能导致其他工种的返工。本模块帮助现场主管建立科学的安装顺序规划，确保各工种有序配合。

## 核心论点 / 洞察
In cleanroom biosafety equipment installation, the highest-cost rework events originate from violating the structural-before-mechanical-before-electrical sequence — electrical conduit routed before structural setting prevents proper anchor placement.

## 参考素材
- Recommended installation sequence: 1) Structural framing and wall opening preparation → 2) HVAC ductwork and damper installation → 3) Mechanical equipment placement and anchoring → 4) Electrical conduit and cable tray routing → 5) Control system wiring → 6) Interlock system configuration → 7) Integrated commissioning
- Handover checkpoint documentation: pre-handover inspection form (minimum 48 hours before next trade begins), punch list sign-off by both outgoing and incoming trade supervisors
- Buffer zone management: minimum 1,500 mm clear access zone maintained during equipment placement phase
- Critical path items: door frame installation (must complete before drywall/sealing), control panel mounting (requires 800 mm clear access), final pressure test (requires 24-hour no-work zone)
