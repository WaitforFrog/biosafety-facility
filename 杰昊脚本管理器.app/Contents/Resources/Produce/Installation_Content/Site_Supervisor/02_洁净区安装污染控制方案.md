# Cleanroom Installation Contamination Control: Implementing Traffic Control and Protective Protocols During Biosafety Equipment Deployment

## 适用受众
Site_Supervisor

## 写作角度说明
洁净区安装期间的污染控制是生物安全设备安装的核心要求之一。现场主管需要制定并执行严格的进入管控、工具清洁和材料进出场程序，防止污染物进入已安装的设备或洁净区域。

## 核心论点 / 洞察
The greatest contamination risk during cleanroom biosafety equipment installation is not the equipment itself but uncontrolled personnel movement — a single improperly dressed worker can invalidate 72 hours of HEPA filter conditioning.

## 参考素材
- Traffic control zones: red zone (equipment staging area), yellow zone (active installation), green zone (completed and sealed areas)
- Personnel entry protocol: cleanroom garment change sequence, HEPA vacuum of all tools before entry, sticky mat replacement frequency (every 50 passes)
- Material entry procedures: all packaging removed in staging area, surface disinfection (70% isopropanol) before entry, minimum 30-minute conditioning in cleanroom before unpacking
- Monitoring requirements: daily particle count logging (minimum 3 locations), visual inspection of seal integrity after each shift
- Containment measures: temporary poly sheeting barriers, negative pressure tent for demolition work, dedicated tool labeling system
