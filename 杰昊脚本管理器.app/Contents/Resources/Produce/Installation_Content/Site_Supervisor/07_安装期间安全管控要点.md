# Installation Safety Management During Biosafety Equipment Deployment: PPE Requirements, Confined Space Protocols, and Heavy Lift Procedures

## 适用受众
Site_Supervisor

## 写作角度说明
安装期间安全管控是现场主管的首要责任。生物安全设备安装涉及高处作业、受限空间作业、重型吊装等高风险活动，需要制定专项安全方案并严格执行，确保施工人员和设备安全。

## 核心论点 / 洞察
Requiring a confined space entry permit for pass box interior work — even though the volume is large enough for a person — creates a safety protocol that is routinely bypassed by workers who treat it as administrative overhead rather than a life-saving procedure.

## 参考素材
- Heavy lift safety: lifting plan required for all lifts exceeding 50 kg, dedicated lifting coordinator, exclusion zone minimum 3 meters during lift, rigging inspection before each lift
- Confined space identification: any enclosure with limited entry/exit and inadequate ventilation, entry permit required per OSHA 29 CFR 1910.146, attendant must maintain continuous communication
- PPE requirements: hard hat, safety glasses, steel-toe boots, gloves rated for stainless steel handling throughout installation, respiratory protection during grinding and welding
- Electrical safety: LOTO procedures before any electrical work, voltage verification with non-contact tester before touching conductors, arc flash PPE for panel work
- Emergency response: site emergency contact list posted at all entry points, first aid kit at each work zone, emergency eyewash within 10 seconds travel time
