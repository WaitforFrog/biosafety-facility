# Electrical Interface Specification Definition: Voltage, Power, and Communication Protocol Requirements for Subcontractor Coordination

## 适用受众
Subcontractor

## 写作角度说明
分包商（电气/HVAC）需要清晰理解生物安全设备的电气接口规格。本模块提供电气接口定义表，包括电压等级、功率需求、通信协议等关键参数，确保分包商的接线工作与设备厂家要求一致，避免接口不匹配导致的返工。

## 核心论点 / 洞察
Routing electrical conduit through the structural opening reserved for the door frame — a common subcontractor error — cannot be corrected without removing the already-installed concrete anchor system.

## 参考素材
- Power supply requirements: 3-phase 380-400V AC, 50 Hz (or single-phase 220-240V AC per model), maximum power consumption per door: 1.5 kW (during inflation), standby: 50 W
- Control voltage: 24V DC for solenoid valves and interlock signals, 24V AC for position sensors
- Communication protocols: Modbus RTU (RS-485, 2-wire half-duplex), Modbus TCP (Ethernet RJ45), BACnet IP optional
- Cable specifications: power cable 3×2.5 mm² shielded, control cable 4×0.75 mm² shielded twisted pair, communication cable Cat6 FTP (for Modbus TCP)
- Terminal block identification: X1: mains power input, X2: interlock outputs, X3: BMS communication, X4: ground/earth
- Grounding requirement: dedicated earth conductor minimum 6 mm², resistance to ground ≤0.1 Ω
