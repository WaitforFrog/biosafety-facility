# Duct Flange Connection and Sealing Standards: HVAC Interface Specifications for Biosafety Airlock Integration

## 适用受众
Subcontractor

## 写作角度说明
风管法兰连接是HVAC分包商配合生物安全设备安装的关键接口。本模块规定风管法兰的连接标准、密封要求和配合尺寸，确保风管系统与设备的气密性连接，避免漏风导致的压差失控。

## 核心论点 / 洞察
Using flexible duct connections longer than 300 mm at the biosafety equipment interface introduces unquantifiable leakage pathways — the flexible section itself becomes a leak source that standard pressure tests cannot isolate.

## 参考素材
- Flange specification: rectangular flange per equipment outlet dimensions (±2 mm tolerance), material: hot-dip galvanized steel 1.5 mm thickness, bolt hole pattern: M8 bolts at 150 mm spacing
- Sealing method: continuous bead of anaerobic flange sealant (e.g., ThreeBond 1215 or equivalent), supplemented with compressed fiber gasket (minimum 3 mm thickness, 10 mm width), bolt torque 15-20 Nm in cross pattern
- Flexible connection: maximum length 150 mm, material EPDM or neoprene-coated fabric, minimum 2 full convolutions, support bracket required within 300 mm of each end
- Duct sizing: velocity ≤12.5 m/s at connection point (to minimize pressure fluctuations), straight duct run minimum 3× duct diameter upstream
- Leakage class: ductwork upstream of biosafety equipment ≤Class 3 per SMACNA HVAC Systems Ducting Standard, tested at 1.5× design pressure
- Coordination requirement: ductwork to be installed after door frame is fully set and leveled, field verify opening dimensions before duct fabrication
