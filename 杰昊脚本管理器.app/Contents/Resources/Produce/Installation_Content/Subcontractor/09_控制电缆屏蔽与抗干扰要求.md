# Control Cable Shielding and EMI Mitigation: Installing Signal Cables to Prevent Electromagnetic Interference with Sensor and Communication Circuits

## 适用受众
Subcontractor

## 写作角度说明
控制电缆屏蔽与抗干扰要求是电气分包商确保控制信号电缆安装质量的核心工作。生物安全设备依赖精确的传感器信号和通信数据，电磁干扰会导致测量误差和通信故障，必须通过正确的电缆敷设和屏蔽接地来预防。

## 核心论点 / 洞察
Grounding the cable shield at both ends — the recommended practice for most installations — can create a ground loop in installations where the grounds at each end are at different potentials, injecting noise rather than rejecting it.

## 参考素材
- Shielding types: individual shielded pairs for analog signals (4-20 mA, 0-10V) → overall braided shield for multi-pair control cables → steel wire armoring (SWA) for buried or mechanical protection areas
- Shield termination: for analog signals → terminate shield at receiving end only (controller input) → insulate shield at sending end (field device) → use 360° shield clamp on the connector
- Separation requirements: maintain minimum 150 mm separation between power cables (>400V) and signal cables → use separate cable trays for power and signal where possible
- Grounding practice: single-point grounding for communication circuits (Modbus RS-485) → ground shield at one end only → use equipotential bonding conductor between grounded points if distance >50 m
- EMI sources to avoid: variable frequency drives (VFD) → welding equipment → large motors during startup → mobile phone chargers near instrumentation
- Testing: measure signal quality with oscilloscope at controller input → verify signal-to-noise ratio ≥40 dB for analog signals → check for ground loop currents with millivolt meter between cable shield and ground
